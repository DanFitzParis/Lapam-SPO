# Solution One-Pager: Restaurant General Manager

**Persona:** Marco — General Manager, 3-location Italian restaurant group, Netherlands  
**Context:** No dedicated HR; responsible for day-to-day operations and hiring at all 3 sites; hires between service shifts  
**Primary use case:** Mobile-first hiring workflow that takes under 10 minutes from posting to candidate contact

---

## Your #1 Challenge

You run three Italian restaurants: Amsterdam, Rotterdam, Utrecht. You're the GM. You're also the scheduler, the recruiter, and the compliance officer. Your owner doesn't want to hire HR until the group hits 5 sites.

You need to hire a kitchen porter and two servers in the next two weeks. You don't have time to sit at a computer for 47 steps. You post the job on Indeed during your lunch break, respond to applications by email when you remember, and interview people between lunch and dinner service.

Last week, you had three strong candidates apply. By the time you saw their applications (two days later), two of them had already taken jobs elsewhere. The one who was left didn't show up for the interview. You're now a week into the hiring process and still haven't filled the role. The kitchen is short-staffed. Your head chef is covering porter duties. It's not sustainable.

The Dutch labour market is tight right now. You can't afford to be slow. But every hiring tool you've tried feels like it was designed for a 200-person HR department, not a restaurant GM working from his phone between services.

---

## How Lapam-ATS Solves It

### Post a Job in 5 Minutes from Your Phone

You open Lapam-ATS on your phone during the 3pm lull between lunch and dinner service.

1. **Select role template:** "Server" (pre-populated job description, standard screening questions)
2. **AI drafts the description in 10 seconds:** You edit the hours (Friday and Saturday evenings, part-time) and pay rate (€13.50/hour)
3. **Add two screening questions:**
   - "Do you have experience with Italian wine service?" (Yes/No)
   - "Can you work Friday and Saturday evenings?" (Yes/No)
4. **Publish:** The system posts to Indeed and generates Google for Jobs structured data within 15 minutes. It also generates a **QR code**.

You print the QR code and tape it to the restaurant's front window. Candidates walking past can scan it with their phone and apply on the spot. You've posted the job in **5 minutes** without sitting at a computer.

---

### See Applications the Moment They Come In

A candidate walking past the Amsterdam restaurant scans the QR code at 4pm. The application loads on her phone instantly. No login. No account creation. She completes it in **3 minutes**:

- Name, mobile number, email
- Availability: part-time
- Answers to the two screening questions: Yes (wine service experience), Yes (Friday/Saturday availability)
- GDPR consent checkbox

She submits. Within **2 minutes**, she receives an automated SMS acknowledgement: *"Thanks for applying to Amsterdam Server at Marco's Trattoria. We'll review your application and be in touch within 24 hours."*

You're in the kitchen prepping for dinner service. Your phone buzzes. You open Lapam-ATS. The application is there. The candidate answered both screening questions favourably. You can see her contact details and availability on one screen.

You tap **"Send Interview Invitation"** and propose three time slots:
- Tuesday 3pm
- Wednesday 11am
- Wednesday 3pm

The candidate receives the options via SMS. She selects **Wednesday 11am**. Both of you receive confirmation messages. The system sends a reminder to both of you 24 hours before the interview.

You've gone from **application to scheduled interview in under 2 minutes** — all from your phone, without leaving the kitchen.

---

### No Desktop Steps. No "Log In Later."

Every tool you've tried before required a desktop step: "Log in to the admin panel to review applications." You don't sit at a desk. You're on your feet for 12 hours a day. If it's not mobile, you won't use it.

Lapam-ATS is **mobile-first by design**:
- Post a job: mobile
- Review applications: mobile
- Send interview invitations: mobile
- Accept or reject candidates: mobile
- Send offer letters: mobile (digital offer, candidate accepts with a tap)

No desktop required. No "I'll do it when I get home" — which means it never gets done. You manage your entire hiring workflow from your phone during the gaps between services.

---

### Speed Wins. Candidates Don't Wait.

The candidate who applied at 4pm got a response from you by 5pm (interview invitation sent). She accepted the Wednesday 11am slot by 5:30pm. By the time she's home from work that evening, she has a confirmed interview.

She applied to five other restaurants that same afternoon. Four of them didn't respond for two days. By the time they sent her an email saying *"Thanks for your interest, we'll be in touch,"* she'd already accepted your interview. She's now emotionally committed to your restaurant because you were first.

In hospitality, **speed wins**. The candidate who waits 3 days for a response takes another job. Lapam-ATS makes you the fastest responder — automatically.

---

### One Platform for Three Locations

You manage hiring for Amsterdam, Rotterdam, and Utrecht. With the informal stack, you were posting three separate jobs to Indeed, responding to three separate email inboxes, tracking candidates in three separate (mostly mental) lists.

With Lapam-ATS, you see **all three locations in one app**:
- Amsterdam: 1 open role (Server), 3 applications, 1 interview scheduled
- Rotterdam: 1 open role (Kitchen Porter), 2 applications, 0 interviews scheduled
- Utrecht: 0 open roles

You can switch between locations with one tap. When a candidate applies to Rotterdam, you see it in the same interface as Amsterdam applications. When your owner asks you *"How many open roles do we have across the group?"* you can answer immediately.

You're managing hiring for three locations without it feeling like three separate jobs.

## Proof Point: Faster Hiring = Less Overtime Cost

You're currently short one kitchen porter and two servers across the three locations. Your head chef is covering porter duties. Your two remaining servers are working extra shifts and burning out. You're paying **overtime** to cover the gaps.

Assume each unfilled role costs you:
- 10 hours of overtime per week at time-and-a-half (€13.50 × 1.5 = €20.25/hour)
- 10 hours × €20.25 = **€202.50 per week per unfilled role**
- 3 unfilled roles × €202.50 = **€607.50 per week**

If Lapam-ATS reduces your average time-to-fill from **3 weeks to 1 week** (faster candidate engagement, no applications lost in email, instant mobile workflow), you save:
- 2 weeks × €607.50 = **€1,215 per hiring cycle**

Lapam-ATS costs you **€149/month for 3 locations** (€50/location/month). That's **€1,788 per year**. One faster hiring cycle pays for the tool for the year. Every subsequent cycle is pure savings.
