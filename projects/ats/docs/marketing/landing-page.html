The Problem

Your hiring process is invisible

You're managing 5, 10, 20 locations — and you have no idea which ones have staffing crises until a manager calls you in panic. The informal stack fails when you scale.

No visibility across locations

You can't see which locations have open roles, how many applications are in progress, or which pipelines are stalled — until someone calls you. No dashboard. No early warning. Decisions are reactive.

Candidates take another job first

Hospitality candidates apply to 4–6 jobs on mobile within an hour. If you take 2–3 days to respond because applications are stuck in email, they've already accepted another offer. Speed wins.

Compliance audit risk

GDPR consent not captured. Right-to-work checks on paper with no audit trail. Spreadsheets and WhatsApp groups can't survive a compliance audit. Fines: £45K–£60K per illegal worker.

The Solution

Built for multi-location hospitality groups
