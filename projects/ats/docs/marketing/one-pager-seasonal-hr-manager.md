# Solution One-Pager: Seasonal Hotel Group HR Manager

**Persona:** Claire — Head of HR, 6 coastal hotel properties, UK  
**Context:** Sole HR professional managing extreme seasonal workforce swing (80 staff off-season → 450 staff peak season)  
**Primary use case:** Rebuilding the workforce from scratch every spring

---

## Your #1 Challenge

You close your properties in November with 80 skeleton staff. You reopen in late March and need 450 people by mid-June. Every spring, you rebuild the entire workforce from scratch — and you spend three weeks manually ringing through a spreadsheet of 200 past workers to find out who's available.

Two of them have moved. Forty don't answer. Twenty say yes. The rest, you source cold from Indeed and Hospitality Online. By the time you've filled 60% of your roles, it's mid-April and you're behind. The good workers you hired two years ago? You have no system to re-engage them quickly. You're starting from zero every single year.

The seasonal hiring window is February to May. If you're still scrambling in April, you're opening understaffed — and that shows up in guest reviews, team burnout, and summer revenue.

---

## How Lapam-ATS Solves It

### Seasonal Talent Pool CRM: Your Past Workers Are Already in the System

When a seasonal employee departs at contract close in October, you tag them in Lapam-ATS:
- **"Re-hire eligible"** — performed well, wants to return
- **"Conditional re-hire"** — acceptable but not first choice
- **"Do not re-engage"** — poor performance or conduct issue

"Re-hire eligible" workers are added to your organisation's talent pool with separate talent pool consent (GDPR-compliant, 12-month consent lifecycle).

In early February — three months before opening — you open Lapam-ATS and send a **batch re-engagement message** to all 200+ past workers:

> *"We're opening again in late March. Interested in coming back this summer? Confirm your availability here."*

The message includes a link to a short availability form (no login required):
- Which dates are you available?
- Which property do you prefer?
- Which role? (housekeeper, front desk, restaurant server, kitchen porter, bar staff)

Within two weeks, 120 of your 200 past workers respond. 80 confirm full availability. 40 confirm partial availability (specific weeks or properties only).

You've just re-hired **60% of your summer roster** before you've even posted a job to Indeed. That's three weeks of your life back. And the people you re-hired are already trained — they know the properties, they know the systems, they know the standards. First-week productivity is 10x higher than new hires.

The remaining 40% of your roles, you source through new recruitment — but you're starting from a **partially filled roster**, not an empty one.

---

### Fixed-Term Contracts, Digital Onboarding, and Right-to-Work at Scale

You're not hiring permanent staff. You're hiring 200+ people on fixed-term seasonal contracts, many of whom are students, international workers on visas, or local residents who work summers only.

**Fixed-term contract support:** Lapam-ATS handles seasonal contract types. You create a template ("Seasonal Housekeeper, March–October 2026, £11.50/hour"). When you re-engage or hire a worker, the system pre-populates the contract. They review and accept on mobile. No printing. No scanning.

**UK right-to-work verification at scale:** Every UK hire requires right-to-work verification before their first shift. With 200+ new starters, you cannot manually check passports and BRPs. Lapam-ATS sends each new hire a mobile link (no login required). They submit their share code or document via GBG IDVT integration. The system returns a pass/fail result and creates an immutable, timestamped audit record. If the check covers a time-limited permission (visa, BRP), the system schedules an alert 30 days before expiry.

You now have **audit-ready right-to-work records for every hire**, stored centrally, with automated expiry tracking. If UKVI audits you mid-season, you can produce every record in under 5 minutes.

**Bulk onboarding:** You're onboarding 50–100 people in the same week. Lapam-ATS allows batch document collection: send a single onboarding link to 50 new hires. They complete their contracts, bank details, emergency contacts, uniform sizes, dietary requirements — all on mobile. You review and approve in bulk. No chasing individuals.

### Off-Season Pipeline Maintenance: Build Next Year's Talent Pool During the Quiet Months

November to February, your properties are closed or running skeleton operations. You have no active recruitment. But you're getting walk-in enquiries, email expressions of interest, and referrals from your current staff.

With Lapam-ATS, you capture these enquiries into the **talent pool** during the off-season. When February arrives, you already have 30–50 warm prospects tagged and ready — on top of your 200 past workers.

This turns your off-season from dead time into **passive pipeline building**. You're not scrambling in March; you're executing a plan you started building in November.
